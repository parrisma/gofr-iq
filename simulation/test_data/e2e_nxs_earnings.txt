Nexus Software (NXS) Reports Record Q4 Earnings on AI Chip Demand

SAN JOSE, Feb 9 -- Nexus Software Inc. (ticker: NXS) reported fourth-quarter
revenue of $4.2 billion, beating analyst estimates by 12%, driven by surging
demand for its semiconductor design tools used in artificial intelligence chip
development.

CEO Maria Chen said on the earnings call that enterprise customers are
accelerating adoption of the company's EDA platform for next-generation AI
accelerator design. "Every major chipmaker is now using NXS tools for their
AI roadmap," Chen said.

The company's AI-related revenue grew 45% year-over-year, now representing
38% of total sales. Gross margins expanded to 82%, up from 78% a year ago.

NXS raised its full-year 2026 guidance to $17.5 billion in revenue, above
the consensus estimate of $16.8 billion. The stock rose 8% in after-hours
trading.

Analysts at Goldman Sachs maintained their Buy rating, citing NXS's dominant
position in the semiconductor design tool market and its exposure to the
secular AI infrastructure buildout.
