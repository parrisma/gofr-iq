# Cash Equities Brokerage Client Profiles

## Top 5 Client Types

### 1. Long-Only Asset Managers
- **Trades**: Large-cap, mid-cap equities; index constituents; low turnover
- **Mandates**: Benchmark-relative (beat S&P 500, MSCI World); ESG/SRI constraints; sector limits
- **Horizons**: 6-18 months holding periods
- **News Interest**: Earnings surprises, dividend changes, index rebalancing, ESG controversies, management changes, M&A targets, sector rotation signals

### 2. Hedge Funds (L/S Equity)
- **Trades**: Pair trades, event-driven, high conviction single names; higher turnover
- **Mandates**: Absolute return; gross/net exposure limits; factor neutral
- **Horizons**: Days to months; catalyst-driven
- **News Interest**: Activist campaigns, earnings whispers, short interest spikes, regulatory actions, insider transactions, credit events, options flow anomalies

### 3. Quantitative/Systematic Funds
- **Trades**: Factor-based (value, momentum, quality); statistical arbitrage; basket trades
- **Mandates**: Factor exposure targets; tracking error budgets; liquidity constraints
- **Horizons**: Intraday to weeks; rebalance-driven
- **News Interest**: Factor regime shifts, volatility events, correlation breakdowns, index changes, liquidity disruptions, data releases (payrolls, CPI)

### 4. Pension Funds / Sovereign Wealth
- **Trades**: Very large blocks; passive/semi-passive; transitions
- **Mandates**: ALM-driven; long duration; low cost; responsible investment policies
- **Horizons**: Years; strategic allocation shifts
- **News Interest**: Central bank policy, demographic trends, geopolitical stability, infrastructure spending, climate policy, corporate governance reforms

### 5. Family Offices / UHNW
- **Trades**: Concentrated positions; tax-aware; illiquid/private crossover
- **Mandates**: Wealth preservation; intergenerational; idiosyncratic preferences
- **Horizons**: Variable; opportunistic
- **News Interest**: Pre-IPO/SPACs, private-to-public events, tax law changes, estate planning triggers, passion investments (sports teams, luxury), founder-led companies

## Reverse Engineering News Relevance

| Factor | Maps to News Type |
|--------|------------------|
| Sector allocation | Sector-specific headlines |
| Geographic mandate | Regional market news |
| ESG constraints | Controversy/sustainability stories |
| Benchmark | Index constituent news |
| Turnover rate | Time-sensitivity of alerts |
| Position concentration | Single-stock deep dives |
| Catalyst focus | Event calendars, filings |
